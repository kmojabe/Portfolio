Rabbit Hole Rescue

Reorganized user history as a tree structure with the parent/children relationships based on user's browsing habits

Rabbit Hole Rescue is an extension that lets you record your Web browsing sessions at the click of a button. When you're logged in to your account, you can save these sessions, and view them on a Web page in the form of a tree diagram. This way, you can see the paths your browsing took, and how you ended up at any given page.

Data visualization application that allows users to view population with respect to time.

Population Bubbles uses D3.js to parse population data from the World Bank between the years 1960 to 2017. The radius of the bubble is proportional to size of the countries population respective to the total population.

Health Portal

Portable health monitoring system.

Health Portal is a small scale prototype that demonstrates an easier method to manage and harness health data. We do so by using sensors to monitor body parameters and transmit that data to a server. Users can interact with their data through an intuitive user interface and view their personalized health information. This enables health conscious users to understand what health measures they need to take. Such personalized health reports presented in an intuitive format circumvent laborious paperwork.

We designed a custom PCB to communicate with the sensors

At the core of Health Portal is an STM microcontroller that communicates with a variety of sensors through I2C, UART, and a custom 1-wire protocol. The microcontrollers sends health data to a Raspberry Pi via I2C. The RPi controls the tablet, user's interface, and communicates that data to our website's backend. Users can view their health data on our website.
